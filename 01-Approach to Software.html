We believe in keeping things simple

One of the ways this shows is in our approach to software development. For us keeping it simple means focussing on one thing and doing it well, each of our software products is designed with this in mind. Most software products on the market have many functions built into a single application. This means they get complicated both technically under the hood, and complicated from the user experience.

At WildGums we take a totally different approach:

  • We identify the functional requirements our users are trying to fill
  • We focus on a single requirement
  • We build an application (module) to deliver that function only
    • In a simple to use
    • Feature rich experience
    • That empowers our users to make better more informed decisions quickly
